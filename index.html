Privacy Policy for Pixel AutoStamp Effective Date: June 2, 2026 Pixel AutoStamp respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the Pixel AutoStamp mobile application handles user data. 1. App Overview Pixel AutoStamp is a local photo utility app for Android devices. The app detects new photos taken on the device and creates a stamped copy of the photo based on the user’s selected stamp settings. The app is designed to work fully on the user’s device. 2. Information We Collect Pixel AutoStamp does not collect, store, sell, or share personal information. The app does not require users to create an account and does not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, location data, contact lists, payment information, or identity information. 3. Photos and Media Access Pixel AutoStamp may request access to photos, images, and media files on your device so it can detect newly saved camera photos and create stamped copies. Photos are processed locally on your device only. Pixel AutoStamp does not upload your photos to any server, cloud service, or third-party system. 4. Local Processing All image detection, stamping, and saving functions are performed locally on the device. The app does not use cloud processing, external servers, remote databases, analytics platforms, advertising SDKs, or third-party tracking tools. 5. Permissions Used Pixel AutoStamp may request the following Android permissions depending on your device and Android version: Photos / Images / Media Access Used to read newly saved photos and create stamped copies. Notification Permission Used to show app status, processing notifications, or foreground service notifications where required by Android. Foreground Service Permission Used to allow the app to monitor for new photos and process them reliably in the background, subject to Android system rules. Storage / Media Write Access Used to save stamped copies of photos to the device. Pixel AutoStamp uses these permissions only for the core photo-stamping functionality of the app. 6. Data Sharing Pixel AutoStamp does not share user data with third parties. The app does not sell user data. The app does not transfer photos, media files, or any personal information outside the device. 7. Advertising and Analytics Pixel AutoStamp does not include advertising SDKs. Pixel AutoStamp does not include analytics SDKs. Pixel AutoStamp does not track user behavior for marketing, profiling, or advertising purposes. 8. Children’s Privacy Pixel AutoStamp is not specifically directed toward children under the age of 13. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since the app does not collect personal information from any user, no children’s personal information is collected, stored, or shared. 9. Data Retention Pixel AutoStamp does not retain personal data on external systems. Stamped photos are saved locally on the user’s device. The user controls their own photos and may delete them at any time using their device’s gallery, file manager, or other photo management tools. 10. Security Pixel AutoStamp is designed to protect user privacy by keeping processing local to the device. Because the app does not upload or transmit user photos or personal data, the risk of external exposure is minimized. Users should still protect their own device using standard security practices such as screen lock, software updates, and secure backups. 11. User Control Users can control app permissions through Android system settings. Users may revoke photo, media, notification, or other permissions at any time. Some app features may stop working if required permissions are disabled. Users may also uninstall Pixel AutoStamp at any time. 12. Changes to This Privacy Policy This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes to the app, Android platform requirements, or legal requirements. Any updated version will be posted at the same Privacy Policy URL with a revised effective date. 13.
